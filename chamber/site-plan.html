Website Planning Document

Site Name: Banose Nigeria

Description: The name reflects the company’s Nigerian roots and its dedication to producing and distributing pure, natural honey. It conveys trust, purity, and a deep connection to nature and traditional Nigerian values.

Site Purpose:

The Banose Nigeria website serves as a platform to showcase the company’s natural honey products, provide educational content about the health benefits of honey, and offer a simple and secure way for customers to place orders or make inquiries. It also includes testimonials, certifications, and contact details to build trust with customers.

Scenarios for Banose Nigeria Website:

1. Where can I buy Banose Nigeria’s natural honey products?
2. What makes Banose honey natural and different from others on the market?

Color Schema:

The Banose Nigeria website uses a warm, natural honey-inspired color palette to reflect the brand’s essence of purity, trust, and nature:

1. Goldenrod (#DAA520) – Used as the primary color for text and branding elements. Represents the rich golden hue of natural honey.
2. Honey Yellow (#FFD54F) – Applied as the background color for all headings to create a vibrant and eye-catching effect.
3. Cream White (#FFF8E1) – Used as the main background for the site and borders to ensure a clean and fresh look.
4. Light Honey Cream (#FFF3CD) – Used as a background color for content blocks and containers to give a subtle warmth.
5. Earthy Brown (#5D4037) – Used for body text and other interface elements to convey warmth and natural roots.
6. Dark Brown (#4E342E) – Used specifically for paragraph text to ensure strong readability with a natural tone.

Typography:

These fonts were selected to reflect a natural, trustworthy, and friendly brand:
1. Header Font: Poppins - Modern and bold sans-serif used for titles and navigation.
2. Body Font: Open Sans - Clean and readable font used throughout the content.
3. Accent Font: Pacifico - A script font used sparingly for branding, such as product names or slogans.
